Requirements for Engineering

Stream to Choose at +2 level:

Take Science stream with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and English at the +2 level.

Subjects to focus:

The most important subjects to focus on are Maths and Physics as it forms the base for the engineering curriculum. Chemistry at the higher secondary level also has importance. Students should give importance to other subjects as well to obtain a high aggregate percentage.

After graduating from high school, prospective engineers will need to earn a bachelor’s degree from a school accredited by the Accreditation Board of Engineering and Technology (ABET). ABET employs over 2,200 engineering experts from the public and private sectors to evaluate key factors in an engineering program, and ensure that they’re up to industry standards. In an era of distance learning options, ABET’s stamp of approval is more important than ever.
